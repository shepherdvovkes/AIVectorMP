How It Works

Step 1: Upload & Commit

Data providers upload vectorized datasets, commit cryptographic root to blockchain.

Step 2: Query & Prove

Buyers submit queries; validators privately search and generate ZK-proofs of correct answers.

Step 3: Verify & Reward

On-chain contract verifies proof, provider/validator receive automatic rewards, and buyers get audit-backed results.
